MMS Missionaries of Mercy and Hope

MMS Missionaries of Mercy and Hope is a Christian religious community dedicated to prayer, evangelization, and works of charity.

Our mission is to serve the poor, bring hope to the vulnerable, and proclaim God’s mercy through faith and action.

Our Mission

  • To live and proclaim God’s mercy
  • To serve the poor and forgotten
  • To strengthen faith through evangelization
  • To bring hope through love and compassion
